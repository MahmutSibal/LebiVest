LEBIVEST was founded on a single belief — that the oldest patterns, drawn with the lightest hand, make the most modern clothes.

We work between two worlds: the geometry of the Anatolian loom and the restraint of contemporary tailoring. Pure linen, wool and silk are cut into quiet, fluid silhouettes, then traced with a gold kilim motif that catches the light only when it should.

01 — The Motif

Geometry with
a memory

The diamond for protection. The hooked border for the road. The running edge for the generations that wove before us. We do not invent these shapes — we carry them forward, one gold thread at a time, onto garments meant to be lived in.

02 — The Cloth

Made to soften,
not to wear out

Every fabric is chosen to age with grace — linen that breaks in, wool that holds its shape, silk that pools and falls. Cut in limited runs, finished by hand, and built to be the piece you reach for in ten years' time.

01

Hand-drawn motifs

Each kilim is mapped by hand before a single thread is sewn, so no two runs are identical.

02

Natural fibres only

Pure linen, virgin wool and silk — traceable, breathable, and chosen to last decades.

03

Limited by intent

Small, considered collections. We make less, and we make it to be kept.
